Sports, Snow Grades, and Sixers Suspense

Welcome to Yesterday in Philly — your no-B.S. rundown of the latest city news. Here’s what mattered Sunday and early Monday. Grab a coffee, check your pipes for leaks, and let's run it back.

Sixers Drama: One Star In, One (Big) Out

  • Sixers fans, brace yourselves: Joel Embiid's not heading to the NBA All-Star Game (he’s recovering from injury), leaving Tyrese Maxey as the solo Philly rep. Plenty of snub-energy already brewing.
  • As if that’s not enough, Paul George's 25-game drug suspension (yes, you read that right) is the talk of the fans — frustration is strong, and nobody’s sugarcoating it. Reaction here.
  • On the bright side: women’s pro hoops came back to Philly after decades, and plenty of locals packed the house for it. Trend to watch?

Weather & City Stuff: Water Mains, Icy Roads, and Frozen 'Nope'

  • Several water main breaks over the weekend left parts of Philly with slick, dangerous roads but, mercifully, not much snow — the nor’easter missed us but hit Jersey and Delaware with wind and bitter cold (details).
  • City officials have a blunt reminder: Don’t be the person trying to walk or skate on frozen rivers right now (promise, it's not worth it).
  • Still talking about snow: How’d Philly do on snow removal? (Let's just say, opinions are—wait for it—mixed.)

Eagles Offseason Shenanigans

  • After the Vic Fangio almost-retired scare, the Eagles reportedly bounced between Jim Schwartz and Jonathan Gannon for DC. For now, it's Sean Desai out and Fangio (still) in. Plus: thoughts on Sean Mannion’s hire if you’re deep in Birds Twitter.

Quick Hits

  • Black History Month’s centennial is fueling new education push amid the anti-DEI storm (more here).
  • Barnes Foundation's Free First Sunday was packed with family-friendly arts and culture. Next one’s March, so mark your calendar.
  • For car nerds: The Philly Auto Show is back with the expected crowds.

Stay warm. Don’t skate on the Schuylkill. And yes, check if your street is still a salt-slush mess before you move your car.
