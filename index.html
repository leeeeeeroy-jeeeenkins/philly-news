Yesterday in Philly

Public Schools: Not Exactly a Vote of Confidence

In case you needed a reminder of how people feel about Philly’s public schools: a new poll found only 8% of Philly voters think public education here is headed in the right direction. Most folks laid blame on funding, but concerns range from teacher support to outdated facilities. If you’re hoping for a local election season where education isn’t a topic—good luck.

Phillies Stay Hot, Braves Feeling the Heat

The Phillies aren’t letting up: they snagged two wins over the Mets to stay right on the Braves’ heels in the NL East (recap). Speaking of heat, Kyle Schwarber came through again with his 30th homer, making himself (and your group chat) impossible to ignore.

Sixers Free Agency Watch: Eyes on John Collins?

If you’ve already moved on to the Sixers’ summer plans, there’s chatter that the team is interested in John Collins. The free agency primer lays out more targets, but for now: don’t be shocked if the roster has a new look come September.
