Big Moves, Stormy Nights, and Transit Messes

Yesterday in Philly: August 14, 2025

Transit Drama (Again)

Still think SEPTA’s meltdown is just background noise? Things are getting real: the Pa. Senate passed a GOP-led transit plan that’s supposed to save SEPTA from chopping routes, but it has a long fight ahead in the House. Meanwhile, planners keep warning that if service gets cut, traffic will get a lot uglier (think city-wide gridlock), and families are sweating potential student commute chaos in September (here’s what the district is trying to do about it).

Rizzo Statue Saga (For Real This Time)

The city is officially giving the Frank Rizzo statue back to the group that originally donated it. If you’re new here: the statue's been sitting in a storage lot since its 2020 removal after protests, and now it’ll head for restoration and relocation—just not back to Center City. The Art Commission voted yes, and it’s a done deal (more here).

Schools & Teachers Holding On—for Now

Philly’s public school teachers are showing up for the first day of school later this month, but don’t get too comfortable—a strike could hit in September (here’s what’s at stake). Factors: contract talks dragging, SEPTA uncertainty, and, y’know, the usual payroll drama.

Floods, Freaky Lights, and Your Commute

Last night’s storms brought flash floods and serious headaches on roads across the region. If you saw an eerie blue light in the sky, you weren’t hallucinating—it was probably a rocket launch from Florida that caught a lot of eyes (pics here).

Bellevue’s Fresh Look & Other Openings

The Bellevue Hotel just finished a major design overhaul—think old-school Philly meets modern bougie. Even if you’re not booking a room, the public spaces are a level up. Over in Spring Garden, Sunflower Fest returns in September. Mark your calendar for snacks, music, and a ridiculous number of selfie opportunities.

Sports: Preseason Everything

The Eagles are slogging through uneven joint practices with the Browns (practice notes). Meanwhile, the Sixers’ 2025 NBA Cup schedule just dropped (group play details), in case you’re tracking all things preseason/pointless bravado.

Quick Hits

  • Thinking of moving? Here’s a real guide to East Passyunk.
  • And don’t forget: yesterday was Philly’s last 8pm sunset of the year. The long evenings are numbered.
