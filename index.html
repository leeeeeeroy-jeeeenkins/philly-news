Sixers Shook, City Still Icy, and Eagles Peek Far Ahead

Welcome to February—Paul George is out, snow still won’t melt, and the Eagles are already plotting 2026.

Sixers Hit With a Paul George Earthquake

  • If you blinked: Paul George got slapped with a 25-game suspension for violating the NBA’s anti-drug program. Whatever the details, this is a legit gut-punch with real luxury tax and playoff implications. Suddenly, the trade deadline matters a lot more. Embiid’s back, but the vibes? Not immaculate.

Eagles: Draft Dreams & a Look Into the (Far) Future

  • The 2026 Eagles roster could look very different; yes, you read that year right. Meanwhile, draft season heats up: here’s 12 offensive and 12 defensive prospects to keep an eye on during the Senior Bowl. If you’re desperate for hope: at least the front office is looking way, way ahead.

City Still Digging Out—And It’s About to Get Weirder

  • We’re nearly a week out from the storm and piles of snow are still eating our parking spots. The city’s getting a middling report card for cleanup, and there’s a nor’easter aiming for us this weekend. Also: the cold’s been so gnarly multiple water mains broke yesterday and even firefighting turned into an ice sculpture in North Philly.

City Life: History, Black History Month, and Actual Fun

  • Black History Month starts strong: the Free Library has a full slate of events throughout the city—maybe check out Cecil B. Moore or catch some live music.
  • Also, the removal of the President's House slavery exhibits is stirring up fresh outrage; advocates say this move is like “pulling pages out of a history book with a razor.”
  • Meanwhile, Penn’s Landing is hosting free ice skating if you need a reason to leave the house (or want to see who’s not afraid of frostbite).

Quick Hits

  • Philly Auto Show is coming back for 2026—mark your calendars if you love shouting "yo, that’s a nice car" every 30 seconds.
  • Wreath-laying at the Liberty Bell celebrated National Freedom Day.
  • Did you hear the tiny earthquake in Jersey? If you felt it, congrats: you probably just wanted an excuse for your coffee spill.

That’s yesterday in Philly. See you on the ice (or under a blanket).
