Yesterday in Philly

Subject: Winter weather & city plans, Eagles get real

Good morning, Philly.

Here’s what you missed while you were avoiding Broad Street potholes:

Winter Is Coming (Seriously This Time)

Bundle up — the first real winter storm of the season is hitting Tuesday. Expect a mix of rain, sleet, and slush, with some actual snow likely (yes, in the city limits). Monday stays cold and dry, but if you’ve been putting off clearing your steps or prepping your ride, don’t wait. More details from AccuWeather here.

I-95 & TRANSIT: Plan Your Moves

Another week, another heads-up about overnight lane closures on I-95 in South Philly for construction. Expect detours after 9 p.m. most nights. Plus, PATCO is changing its evening schedule for weekday nights, so double-check your train times if you’re a late commuter.

City Priorities & Policy Moves

  • The Move-In Affordability Plan launches this week — aiming to help renters by capping up-front move-in costs. Might save you some cash if you’re looking for a new place.
  • City planners want your feedback for Philly’s next big master plan update. Speak up now, or don’t complain later when there’s another soulless condo on your block.
  • If you’re worried about winter utility shut-offs, check out this guide on moratoriums and how to keep the heat on.

Eagles: The Vibes Are Off

That loud thudding sound you heard was the Birds dropping their latest vs. the Bears — with some scary déjà vu for fans remembering last year’s collapse. Deep-dive on the loss and snap counts here. Prognosticators are just as nervous: Week 13 picks breakdown who (still) believes.

Sixers Watch

Good-ish news: Joel Embiid might finally be back after 9 games out, "questionable" for the Hawks matchup. In the meantime, Tyrese Maxey is doing everything while Nick Nurse juggles rookie minutes — here’s how that looks on the stat sheet.

Quick Hits

  • Gadaleto's Seafood Market is open, if you want fresh fish in Delaware County and community vibes.
  • Looking for pet pics with Santa? Here’s a cute way to support Animal Welfare Association.
  • Philly friends are on the ultimate quest for the city’s best SportsTown and made a web series about it.

Stay safe, stay warm, and if you’ve got salt, you’ll be the hero of your block.
