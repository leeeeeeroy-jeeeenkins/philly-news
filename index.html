Bucks County: Explosion at Nursing Home

A major gas explosion hit a nursing home in Bristol Township yesterday: 2 confirmed dead, several more hurt and some still unaccounted for. Crews are still digging out the scene. If you have family at Silver Lake, officials say to reach out ASAP for updates. Local hospitals are treating multiple victims (full story).

City Moves & Philly Life

  • Lights on: Philly has finished swapping out 130,000 streetlights for new LEDs. The city says it’ll save energy and brighten up those dim blocks, but let’s see if it also keeps you from tripping on that endless South Philly sidewalk construction.
  • Big lawsuit: Philadelphia is suing giant drug companies and benefit managers, claiming they’re jacking up insulin prices unfairly. For anyone dealing with diabetes in the city (or trying to keep a kid’s prescriptions refilled), this could someday matter to your wallet.
  • Rental headaches: The city just hit a contractor with over 80 stop-work orders for allegedly setting up a fake company to duck penalties—basically, city is trying (again) to get tough on shady developers.
  • Gen Z job market not so easy: Philly’s recent grads are still having a tough time landing gigs with decent pay, despite plenty of new faces at Center City co-working spots.

Transit, Travel, & What’s Open
