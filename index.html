Yesterday in Philly

Subject: Winter Hype, SNAP Drama, and Sewage Splash

It's getting colder, messier, and trickier to afford groceries. Welcome to Philly in October.

🧊 City in a Deep Freeze?

If you’re into winter guesswork, Fox 29 has already dropped their Philly snow forecast. They're calling for bigger storms and more snow than last year. As usual: believe it when you see it, but maybe book a shovel before everyone else panics.

🥦 SNAP Benefits Cliff: November’s Gonna Suck

If you or your neighbors use SNAP, this is not a drill. With the budget fight in D.C. dragging on, federal food assistance is about to dry up. Pennsylvania and New Jersey are now suing the USDA to force the release of November funds (details here). Meanwhile, there’s real anxiety about what happens when the money stops flowing. Here’s where to get help if you need it.

🌊 Sewage in the Schuylkill, Again

It’s almost astonishing: Philly sends billions of gallons of untreated sewage into its rivers every year when it rains hard. Two rivers (Delaware & Schuylkill) and all this gunk—fun! The problem is way bigger than just a bad smell, with health consequences for anyone swimming or fishing. Don’t expect a fix anytime soon.

🚎 Transit & Education: Seniors with Buses

Transportation is now officially a college roadblock: Some Philly high schoolers can’t visit Pennsylvania state schools because they can’t get a ride. Now the schools are chartering buses to make it happen. Also related: one local district could run out of money in months if Harrisburg keeps screwing around with the budget.

🍷 Eats, Drinks, and Barkley’s Beef

  • WineDive returns to Rittenhouse soon — mark your calendars, lovers of casual vino and loud snacks.
  • French onion soup is morphing into pizza and dumplings all over town. Philly Chefs: never bored.
  • Charles Barkley is bringing a steakhouse and cigar lounge to Valley Forge Casino. Keep your jersey clean.

🏈, 🏀, & 🎟 Event Quick Hits

  • Eagles: Still near the top of the NFL power rankings, tinkering with the offense.
  • Sixers: Embiid and Watford are playing, but Paul George (knee) is still out.
  • Veterans Parade is set for Nov. 9 (details), World Cup tickets (here), and plenty more in this week’s event guide.

Quick Shout-Outs / Warnings

  • Deer collisions spike now — drive like you like your bumper (stay safe).
  • City streetlights are getting a $91 million LED upgrade (here's what to expect).

That’s yesterday. Stay warm, keep your snap benefits card handy, and don’t swim in the Schuylkill.
