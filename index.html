SEPTA Strike Threat, Eagles Hold On, & Sixers Knee Drama

Transit

SEPTA’s operators and mechanics just voted to authorize a strike—not the news you want on a Monday. For now, nothing’s shut down, but it’s pressure-time as union leaders keep negotiating. (If you depend on buses, subways, or trolleys, keep an eye out: WHYY, Fox29, 6ABC.)
