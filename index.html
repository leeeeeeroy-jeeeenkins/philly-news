Yesterday in Philly

Friday’s Forecast: Ferris Wheels, Road Closures, and New Renter Rules

Transit & Streets

  • No Kings & Bike Ride = Road Closures: Center City’s going to be a maze Saturday thanks to the annual Philly Bike Ride and a big ‘No Kings 2.0’ protest/rally. Expect detours, especially around Ben Franklin Parkway and Broad. SEPTA buses will reroute, so double-check your commute if you’re heading into town (map & times).
  • SEPTA Silverliner IV Under Review: City Council held a public hearing about those aging Regional Rail cars. If you rely on regional rail and have ever wondered if your train will actually show up, this was for you (details).
  • Crime Down on SEPTA (Somehow): New report says the agency’s crackdown on fare evasion might be driving down crimes on transit (report). Enjoy it while it lasts.

Renters, Read This

  • Voter Reg Info with Leases: Philly is about to start giving all new renters voter registration forms when they sign a lease. Why? Because we have a million people moving in and out every year, and our turnout’s not great (full story).

Local Economy & Biz

  • Jefferson Health Laying Off Hundreds: One of Philly’s biggest hospital networks is cutting 1% of its workforce (about 400 people) citing “financial headwinds.” If you work there, check your email. (details)

Eat & Drink

  • Forest & Main Lands in Fishtown: Ambler’s cult-favorite brewery opens its first Philly taproom on Friday. Expect farmhouse ales, homemade snacks, and zero Main Line vibes. (menu & address)
  • Baker to Watch: Dayna Evans, former food writer, is now behind Downtime Bakery. She went from reviewing restaurants to making killer sourdough in Point Breeze.
  • Insomnia Cookies is coming to PHL Airport for all your late-night (or, let’s be real, anytime) sugar emergencies (when & where).

Culture & Fun

  • Ferris Wheel at the Zoo: Philly Zoo is building a 110-foot-tall Ferris wheel, opening soon as part of its holiday light show (renderings & date).
  • Halloween on the Library Rooftop: Free Library is hosting a Halloween bash on the roof Oct. 30. Plan that costume (info).
  • Abbott Elementary Star Hits Hulu: Lisa Ann Walter filmed her first standup special at Helium—it’s dropping next year (preview).

Sports Corner

  • Sixers Star Paul George Still Healing: He’s back at practice, but not cleared for opening night. Expect more updates “shortly into the regular season.” (injury report)
  • Phils: No Major Offseason Moves? Don’t expect a roster blowup, but there may be one important shakeup (paging Kyle Schwarber). (breakdown)
  • Eagles Health Watch: Key players still banged up ahead of Vikings game. (updates)

Weekend heads-up: Stay aware of traffic and detours, and maybe eat a cookie and take a spin on that new Ferris wheel.
