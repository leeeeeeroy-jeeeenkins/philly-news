Yesterday in Philly

Brandon Graham’s Back, Wawa Drama, and a Super Bowl Run?

Hope you wore your Kelly green—Brandon Graham’s out of retirement, armored truck robbers fumbled a Wawa job, and the NBA season is knocking. Let’s get into what mattered yesterday:

🦅 Brandon Graham Is (Officially) Back

Boss move: Brandon Graham unretired and rejoined the Eagles for what could be a third Super Bowl push. Yes, for real—this was confirmed by basically every outlet (FOX29, 6ABC, WHYY), not just your group chat. The longtime defensive end got his storybook ending—and then decided the story isn’t over. Can he still make an impact at age 37? That’s the question (here’s the analysis).

🏟️ Sports Desk

  • Sixers Hope: Joel Embiid is not on the injury report for opening night (tomorrow!), which is about as good a sign as we get in this city. Paul George is out, if you care about the rest of the NBA.
  • Flyers Watch: Owen Tippett stayed hot with a pair of goals against the Kraken.
  • Union Playoffs: The Union kick off their postseason tonight. No wrong way to get your Philly playoff stress.
  • Monster Jam is returning to the Linc in April if monster trucks are your thing.

🌆 In the City

  • Wawa Mayhem: The FBI is looking for suspects who tried (and failed) to rob an armored truck outside a Northeast Philly Wawa. Check out their photos here—maybe you recognize somebody?
  • Chester County Pharmacy Drama: Investigators say a local pharmacy sold 30,000 doses of an uninspected weight-loss drug. Yes, 30,000. If you’re on a new “miracle” med, double check your source.
  • Kada Scott Update: Investigators suspect accomplices in the death of Kada Scott. There’s a push now to finally demolish the abandoned school where her body was found (here’s the story).
  • Mural Arts: Mural Arts is looking for an artist to create a piece honoring queer activists in the Gayborhood.

🍽 Neighborhood Things

  • Cuba Libre is rolling out Philly’s first GLP-1-friendly restaurant menu (yes, Ozempic diners are a trend now).
  • Geno’s Steaks is going suburban (sort of): They’re opening a shop in Virginia. Prepare yourself for the inevitable cheesesteak discourse.

🗓 What Else We’re Watching

  • Pennsylvania mail ballot requests are open—plan ahead if you want to avoid Election Day chaos.
  • Gov. Josh Shapiro’s memoir comes out in January. Expect plenty of 2028 speculation.

That’s yesterday. Talk tomorrow.
