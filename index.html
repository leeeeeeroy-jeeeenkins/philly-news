SEPTA, Eagles, and Not-So-Subtle City Drama

Your daily Philly news catch-up: SEPTA takes a lap, Eagles survive Dallas drama, and water ice gets stronger.

🚍 SEPTA Service: Back to Normal (Sort of)

A judge just ordered SEPTA to roll back those recent, annoying service cuts. So if your bus or train vanished—good news, it’s coming back. Bad news: the fare hikes stay. If you got overcharged on Monday, check for a refund. Also, for the Eagles home opener, SEPTA’s Sports Express trains are back, at least for the game.

🦅 Eagles: Opening Chaos, Old Foes

League drama is alive: the Eagles took down the Cowboys in a lightning-delayed opener with weather delays, ejections (Jalen Carter didn’t even make it to the first snap [here]), and plenty of banner-waving. Week 1 inactives & analysis are in, and everyone’s already predicting a Super Bowl run (because of course). Jalen Hurts, meanwhile, debuts his Wheaties box for peak Philly breakfast energy. Oh, and allegedly, the Eagles tried to trade for Micah Parsons. Bold.

⚾ Phillies: Another Series Win

Trea Turner came up clutch (again) as the Phils took 2 of 3 from the Brewers (highlights). October’s feeling close.

🏠 Civic Moves

  • A new system is rolling out to fight deed fraud targeting properties of deceased owners—so check your grandma’s house paperwork.
  • Philly’s cash assistance pilot is helping renters avoid eviction—jury’s still out on whether it’ll get expanded.

🍧 Water Ice, But Make It Boozy

Because why not: Rita’s and the Eagles teamed up for new water ice-flavored vodka. Use with caution at the next tailgate.

🏙️ Looking Ahead: Philly’s 250th

Plans are coming out for the America 250 celebration—music, food, extra traffic, and probably a whole lot of fireworks. The hype machine is officially on.

🏖️ And Finally…

  • Atlantic City’s Boardwalk just got ranked #1 in Jersey by Fodor’s, so expect the Shore bragging to ramp up.
  • If you scroll TikTok while in the bathroom: doctors want you to stop (seriously).
  • COVID shot eligibility is easier in PA now, but expect some confusion at the pharmacy.

We’ll see you Monday. Go Birds, tip your bus driver, and maybe check your deed status for real.
