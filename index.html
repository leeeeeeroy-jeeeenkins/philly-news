Yesterday in Philly

SUBJECT: Gas Leaf Blower Drama and Eagles Rumblings

Welcome to Yesterday in Philly—your cheat sheet on what actually went down in the city. Let’s get to it.

City Life: Blower Ban, Utility Bills, & a Bit of Weird

  • Lower Merion’s ban on gas-powered leaf blowers is official, after much neighborly yelling. What’s it mean for you? If you live in Montco, get ready for quieter mornings (maybe) and some annoyed landscapers.
  • Water bills got you steaming? New city legislation is tackling both sky-high utility bills and busted meters. Not fixed yet, but City Council is moving on it.
  • Plymouth Meeting Mall’s getting a glow-up—and a new name. Another dead mall tries to become something else. Keep an eye out if you shop or drive near there.

Sports: Eagles vs. Cowboys, Maxey’s Big Night, and More

  • The Eagles travel to Dallas for Week 12. Five things to watch, from interesting matchups to the latest injuries. Bonus: some teammates rumored to be frustrated, but Hurts says everyone’s good. We’ll see.
  • Sixers update: Joel Embiid sat out vs. the Bucks (knee), and Nick Nurse’s latest lineups didn’t exactly work against the Raptors. But Tyrese Maxey dropped a wild 54 points the game before. If you’re only paying half-attention, he’s turning into a real star.
  • Phillies: There’s silly-season MLB buzz, with trade rumors swirling around Nick Castellanos and talk of a "Field of Dreams" game against the Twins next year.

Philly People & Culture

  • Colman Domingo, West Philly native and actor, will be Temple’s commencement speaker this spring. Upgrade from the usual politicians, honestly.
  • HBO’s Delco crime drama Task just got renewed for a second season. Mark Ruffalo fans (and Delco accent nerds), rejoice.
  • Wanna watch Philly’s zoo light up at night? LumiNature is back. Tickets/info here.
  • Thanksgiving sandwich season is here—see the full Gobbler rundown if you’re obsessed with stuffing on a roll.

Quick Hits

  • Bryn Mawr Birth Center—around for 47 years—is closing next year. More on what happened.
  • A chemical spill caused an evacuation in West Mt. Airy yesterday. Everyone’s safe, but… that’s never what you want to hear before the holidays.
  • Temple University: Student suspended after an alleged sexual assault on campus. Story here.

That’s the recap. Forward to that friend who always asks what’s going on. Back tomorrow.
