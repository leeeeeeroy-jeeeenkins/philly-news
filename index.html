🍳 Delco Scores a Philly All-Day Breakfast Staple

Upper Darby is about to get a taste of Philly’s best hangover cure: Wiz, the North Philly breakfast joint, is moving into Wilson’s Secret Sauce’s old spot on Garrett Road. This means classic, affordable all-day breakfast outside city lines — not just for Temple kids anymore.

🚦 Philly Courts Scam Warning

Getting texts about a ‘toll violation’ from Philadelphia courts? It’s a scam. Don’t click links or send info. Actual court business won’t hit your texts like this. Same goes for a fresh Bucks County scam alert — lots of fake emails making the rounds right now.
