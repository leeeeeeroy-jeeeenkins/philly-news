Subject: Eagles Win Ugly, Brandon Graham Hints at a Return

Miss the Birds game? Hurts and Brown did just enough, while the defense got back to snagging picks — plus, rumors fly about a fan favorite possibly unretiring.

🏈 Eagles Edge Out the Vikings (Stressfully)

If you skipped the game and just checked the score, you’d think it was normal — Eagles 28, Vikings 22. But it was messy. Hurts threw for 326 yards and 3 TDs, mostly by launching it up to A.J. Brown (two touchdowns for him). Things got tight late after some iffy defense and a garbage-time score. Takeaways returned — finally — but the run game is still MIA (stock watch), and the O-line remains beat up. Recap and highlights: What mattered most.

🏀 Sixers: Embiid’s Back, Season Incoming

Joel Embiid joined a "heavy" practice and looked healthy in the team’s preseason finale (full report). The opener is three days away, and it looks like the big man will be good to go. A few young guys — VJ Edgecombe, Adem Bona — turning some heads in camp, too (takeaways).

📰 Around Town

  • Tragic update: The body found behind a school in Germantown has been identified as Kada Scott, the 23-year-old missing woman. Vigil planned; the case is still being investigated.
  • Advocacy: Philly bike advocates rallied at Fairmount Park over the weekend to push for safer streets and remember a rider who was killed last week.
  • Culture: Rousseau’s jungle dreamscapes landed at the Barnes Foundation (retrospective open now).
