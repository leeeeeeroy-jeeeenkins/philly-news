Yesterday in Philly: Snow Watch, Library Fights, and LL Cool J for NYE

Weather's about to flip, SEPTA's rerouting, library drama in North Philly, and LL Cool J is back for New Year's Eve. Stay in the know.

Weather: Snow’s Coming, Apparently

Better dig out your half-broken shovel—forecasts say snow is on for this weekend, with up to a few inches likely for Philly and the usual dice roll for the ‘burbs. Streets shouldn’t be a disaster, but you know how we are with ‘flurries.’

Transit: SEPTA’s Trolley Detours for Months

If you ride the Delaware County trolley routes, brace yourself: weekend service goes to buses instead of trolleys, now through May. Plan for longer trips, more waiting, and—let’s be real—probably some confusion.

City Moves: Drama Over the Future of Cecil B. Moore Library

The fight isn’t over for North Philly’s Cecil B. Moore Library. The library’s old bones need serious work, but the local rep and neighbors can’t agree on whether it should get a big renovation or a total rebuild. Expect more debate (and, possibly, delays) before any construction crews show up.

Events: LL Cool J Headlines Free NYE Show

Swing by the Parkway on December 31 for a free New Year’s Eve concert with LL Cool J. He dropped out of Welcome America this year, but is making it up in time for the countdown. No ticket needed—just show up, and hope it’s not snowing sideways.

Food & Family: CHOP Opens Free Pantry for Kids With Allergies

If you’ve got a kid dealing with food allergies (and the sticker shock at the grocery store), CHOP just opened a pantry to help families get allergen-free basics for free. It’s the first of its kind in Philly, and they’re looking for donations too.

Philly Pride: Mummers Parade Gets Top Parade Spot

USA Today just voted our Mummers Parade the #1 holiday parade in the country. Perfect timing for the 125th anniversary—so polish your umbrella, or your gold spandex, depending on your squad.

ICYMI: Sports & Weekend Fun

  • Football fans: Eagles-Raiders preview—the Birds need a win, and Maxx Crosby is a real concern. Also, key injuries to watch.
  • Ski season plans? Check best lodges near Philly and a guide for families with kids.
  • Need a dose of nostalgia? Harry Potter movie nights at Libertee Grounds, Tuesdays through January.
  • Art on a budget: PAFA holiday pop-up open now, with prints that won't break the bank.

That’s it. Bundle up, plan those trolley detours, and check back tomorrow for whatever fresh chaos Philly throws at us next.
