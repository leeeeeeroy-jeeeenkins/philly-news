SEPTA Funding Crunch, Phillies Sweep, and Eagles Bumps

Yesterday in Philly: why Gov. Shapiro and SEPTA are sounding the alarm, why the Phillies are smiling, and everything that actually matters from the past 24 hours.

🚌 SEPTA: D-Day Is Near

If you ride SEPTA (or care about basic city function), pay attention: Governor Shapiro joined transit officials and regular riders this weekend, ramping up pressure on Harrisburg as we lurch toward a funding cliff. SEPTA says it could face major cutbacks in service and jobs unless state lawmakers pass a lifeline bill, and the deadline is days away. Everyone from the governor to your bus driver is asking the House to act. If you’ve got opinions, now’s the time to yell at your rep.

⚾ Phillies: W is for Wheeler (and Winning Streaks)

The Phillies swept the Rangers, putting another notch in the win column — even as Zack Wheeler threw with less velocity than usual. Concern? Maybe. Panic? Nah — the bullpen just got backup. David Robertson is back (yes, again) so the Phils are bolstering for the long haul.

🦅 Eagles Camp: Injuries, Insights, & One Public Practice

Training camp is deep in the grind. Yesterday was the only public practice at the Linc — so if you’re reading this from home, better luck next year. Highlights: QB Dorian Thompson-Robinson keeps looking decent (notes here), Jahan Dotson is gelling with Hurts (read more), and the defensive shakeups keep coming (Vic Fangio has thoughts). Big scare yesterday: Landon Dickerson got banged up — but A.J. Brown and Jalyx Hunt are back. Camp battle tracker nerds: here’s who’s scrapping for a job.

🏙️ City Notes

  • DA Race is On: Pat Dugan is running against Larry Krasner (again) for Philly DA in 2025. Buckle up for another round of debate about city safety.
  • Protests: Pro-Palestine demonstrators marched through Center City Sunday, drawing crowds and cop presence but few major disruptions.
  • Tech Money Moves: Ben Franklin Tech Partners is moving to Center City — possibly good news for Philly’s startup scene (and anyone needing a fresh pitch meeting spot).
  • Yes, It’s Muggy: If you feel sticky all the time, it’s not in your head. Philly’s dealing with record humidity this summer.
  • Meteor Shower: If you can see past the haze, summer’s best meteor shower peaks this week.
