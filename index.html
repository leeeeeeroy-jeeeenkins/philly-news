Eagles Win Ugly, But It Still Counts

The Eagles eked out a 13-12 win over the Bills, but don't let the W fool you—the Birds had just 16 yards in the second half. Yep, that’s not a typo. The offense looked lost, and the word "incompetence" is being thrown around (not resilience). On the bright side, the pass rush is still deadly (see, Jalen Carter), and the D bailed everybody out, with Buffalo’s MVP missing a wide-open 2-point conversion. Want the play-by-play? Heads Up: Atlantic City Expressway Going Cashless

If you’re heading to the Shore after Jan. 4, know that the Atlantic City Expressway will stop accepting cash at tolls. Get EZ-Pass or be ready for a bill in the mail. (You’ve been warned.)

New Year’s Eve: Security & Concert on the Parkway

Philly’s gearing up for its first-ever NYE concert on the Parkway. Expect big crowds and a heavy security presence—so give yourself extra time if you’re heading downtown.
